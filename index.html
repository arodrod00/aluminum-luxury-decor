What We Build

Aluminum Structures Built for Your Life

From elegant pergolas to fully enclosed patio rooms — every project is custom-designed, permit-ready, and professionally installed.

Modern Pergolas

Custom aluminum pergolas with clean architectural lines. Louvered and fixed-roof options — designed for South Florida's sun and weather.

Terraces

Open-air aluminum terraces that elevate your outdoor living. Durable, stylish, and built for Florida's year-round outdoor lifestyle.

Bathrooms

Custom outdoor aluminum bathrooms — ideal for pool areas and entertainment spaces. Weatherproof, stylish, and built to last.

Patio Rooms

Fully enclosed aluminum patio rooms that extend your living area into the outdoors. Transform an underused space into a year-round room.

Screen Enclosures

High-quality screen enclosures for pools, patios, and lanais. Keep insects out while enjoying natural light and airflow all year.

Storage

Custom aluminum storage structures built to withstand South Florida's climate. Secure, weather-resistant, and completely low-maintenance.

Fences

Durable aluminum fencing that combines security and curb appeal. Rust-free, low-maintenance, and built to last Florida's climate.

Pet Suites

Custom aluminum pet suites — secure, comfortable, and weather-resistant. Give your pets a dedicated space built to last Florida's elements.

Decorative Walls

Custom decorative aluminum wall panels that add style, privacy, and character to any outdoor space. Durable, low-maintenance, and fully customizable.
